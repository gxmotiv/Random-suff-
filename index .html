04Process

A method
rooted in
patience.

I.
Listen
We begin in silence. Before solutions, we seek understanding—of your story, your audience, the atmosphere you wish to inhabit.
II.
Distill
From immersion, essence. We reduce until only the inevitable remains—concept, tone, direction.
III.
Compose
Building in layers. Type, motion, interaction, code—each element tuned to the whole, like instruments in a chamber.
IV.
Reveal
The work goes into the world. We polish until the seams disappear, leaving only the experience.

07Studio
Lisbon Studio38.7°N 9.1°W

We are a small studio by design—twelve people, two cities, one obsession with the details others overlook.

Founded in 2017 by Iris Halberg and Tomás Reyes, Vesper was born from a shared conviction: that the web had grown loud, and that the most interesting work would happen in the quiet. We work with a handful of clients each year—brands, artists, and institutions who understand that craft takes time.

Our team includes designers, engineers, a sound designer, a typographer, and a botanist (long story). We believe diverse perspectives make sharper work.

08Questions

We take on roughly twelve projects per year. We look for clients who value craft over speed, who have a story worth telling, and who are willing to trust the process. Chemistry matters as much as the brief—we want to work with people who are genuinely excited to collaborate, not just hand over a checklist.

Most engagements run between three and six months. Brand identity work tends toward the shorter end; immersive digital experiences often take longer. We don't rush—our best work emerges from careful iteration. If you have a hard deadline in under three months, we're probably not the right studio for you.

Yes, selectively. We've worked with early-stage companies when there's a strong vision and adequate budget to do the work justice. We're not the right fit for rushed MVPs or companies looking for a template to "test the market." We prefer to build things that last.

We work on fixed-fee project basis, not hourly. This allows us to focus on the quality of the outcome rather than the quantity of hours. After an initial conversation, we provide a detailed proposal with scope, timeline, and investment. Most engagements begin at $80k and scale based on complexity and scope.

Absolutely. We frequently collaborate with in-house design and engineering teams. We can lead the project or integrate as an extension of your team—we're flexible about the working relationship. We're also happy to conduct workshops to help align your team around the direction before we begin building.

Yes. We offer retainers for evolution and maintenance, and we stay in touch with all past clients. Many of our relationships span years and multiple projects. We don't believe in "launch and leave"—the best digital experiences are gardens that need tending.

You do, fully. Upon final payment, all intellectual property rights—including design files, code, and assets—transfer to you. We do reserve the right to showcase the work in our portfolio and for award submissions, unless otherwise agreed upon in the contract.

We are technology agnostic, but we favor modern, performant stacks. For frontend, we work with React, Next.js, and Three.js/WebGL. For backend, we use Node, headless CMSs (Sanity, Contentful), and serverless architecture. For design, we use Figma, Cinema 4D, and custom tooling. The tools serve the work, not the other way around.
